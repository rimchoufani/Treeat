# Tree Species Scoring — Treeat

## Ficus macrocarpa
- Cooling score: 9/10
- LAI: 3.43
- PET reduction: up to 5.07°C
- Allergy risk: Low
- VOC emissions: Low
- Best for: Exposed wide streets
- Avg cost Austria: €480

## Michelia alba  
- Cooling score: 7/10
- LAI: 2.8
- PET reduction: up to 3.2°C
- Allergy risk: Low
- VOC emissions: Low
- Best for: Narrow corridors
- Avg cost Austria: €420

## Tilia cordata (Winter Linden)
- Cooling score: 8/10
- LAI: 3.1
- PET reduction: up to 4.1°C
- Allergy risk: Medium (pollen June)
- VOC emissions: Low
- Best for: Boulevard streets
- Avg cost Austria: €380

## Platanus acerifolia (London Plane)
- Cooling score: 8/10
- LAI: 3.6
- PET reduction: up to 4.8°C
- Allergy risk: Medium (seed hairs)
- VOC emissions: Low
- Best for: Wide avenues
- Avg cost Austria: €450

## Betula pendula (Silver Birch)
- Cooling score: 5/10
- LAI: 2.1
- PET reduction: up to 2.3°C
- Allergy risk: HIGH (major allergen)
- VOC emissions: Medium
- Best for: Parks only — avoid streets
- Avg cost Austria: €280
